        Mô tả

        Kagewani is a Japanese anime television series. The series of shorts premiered on 2 October 2015 on Tokyo MX and BS11 as part of the Ultra Super Anime Time programming block. The series was simulcast by Crunchyroll. A second season titled Kagewani: Shō aired from April 1, 2016 to June 23, 2016. Wikipedia
